My Reflections

This was an incredible experience! I learned a lot about taking care of myself during my time as a ranch hand.

I learned the importance of staying hydrated and maintaining a balanced diet. I also learned to innovate and adapt to new situations.

I learned the value of hard work and perseverance. even through challenges and adversities. I grew so much by being pushed past my limits.

I am grateful for the opportunity to go to my uncle's ranch and be a ranch hand.
